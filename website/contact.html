Frequently Asked Questions

When will SIE be available?

We're currently in active development with early access testing underway. Join our early access program to be notified when we launch.

What telematics systems do you integrate with?

We're building integrations with major providers including Samsara, Lytx, and common ELD platforms. Let us know what you use and we'll prioritize accordingly.

How much will SIE cost?

Pricing is still being finalized. Our goal is to make SIE accessible to fleets of all sizes. Early access participants will receive preferred pricing.

Is my data secure?

Data security is paramount. We're implementing industry-standard encryption and compliance measures. Your fleet data remains your property.

Can I try SIE with my own data?

Early access participants will be able to test SIE with their actual fleet data to evaluate its effectiveness before committing.

Who built SIE?

SIE was built by a safety professional who spent years frustrated by the gap between having data and knowing what to do with it. Read our story.
